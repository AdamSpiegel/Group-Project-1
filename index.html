Cocktail & Recipe Generator

    How it Works!

    Welcome to The Cocktail and Recipe Generator! Get fresh ideas for your next meal! Below you will discover a wide variety of randomized coktails and recipes for your enjoyment! All you have to do is choose your liquor and food of choice, and then a variety of drink selections and recipes will produce for you!

    Step 1 - Enter you liquor of choice (by letter) into the first search box. You will see a list generate which includes that ingredient. Highlight your choice and it will auto populate to the search coloum, and click "Search".

    Step 2 - Highlight you beverage of choice in the drop down box. It will also auto populate to the "Choose Your Beverage" search box. Click "Submit" and your cocktail recipe generates!

    Step 3 - Want to pair a meal with that cocktail? Search a food ingredient (by letter) in the "Search by Food Ingredient" box. You will also see a drop down of meal choices, so choose one and click "Search"!

    Step 4 - Finally, click on the meal you would like, and you will see it also auto populate to the "Choose Your Meal" search box. Click "Submit" and presto, you have a recipe to cook!

    Additional Options! - If you change your mind, and would like the closest Restaurants and Breweries available, you have two buttons at the top of the screen which will help you make a different choice!
